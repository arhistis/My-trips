The second time ,I was in Madrid which is a great megalopolis but nos as outstanding as Barcelona.I mean I could visited all ” cool stuff” made by Antoni Gaudi,including Sagrada Familia.He was an unique architecture by it’s “natural” ideas.I also went with my father to see Barcelona vs Atletico Madrid on Nocamp in SuperCopa.(HALA BARCA!!!)
