Amsterdam! My favourite city.

"LEEF EN LAT LEVEN" - Live and let live

Since my short ,but wonderful visit in Amsterdam,I have wondered if that just might be the best place to live.I mean,this awesome capital has excellent quality of life.It mixes the architecture of buildings with the beautiful canals in a special ambiance.

I really have to mention these things: more bikes and fewer cars,great people who make an outstanding atmosphere and a young,cool vibe made by the off-beat cafes and festivals make Amsteram one of the best capital in Europe.Yes,Amsterdam is unique.
