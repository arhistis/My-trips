Croatia

Description

Capital: Zagreb
Official languages: Croatian
Government: Republic
Area: 56,594 km2
Population: 4,284,889

Oh Croatia! I really miss you. Actually I miss your rockbeaches.

Well, I have to admit that this holiday was absolutely perfect.The water was clear but cold.Actually that water used to have on average 17-18 C degrees,so in most of the times my mood turned into a bad one when ever I had to get used with the freezing water. I could practice practice my cliff jumping skills.

I also got the chance to visit Dubrovnik and find out interesting information about Yugoslavia.By the way,Dubrovnik is an elegant and cool stronghold that totally deserved to be visited at least one time.

Zagreb
